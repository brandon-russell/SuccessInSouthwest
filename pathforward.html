What’s Ahead

Champion Every Path to Success – Academics, trades, and the arts for every student.

Keep Our Schools Safe – Strong security to protect students, teachers, and staff.

Restore Respect in Classrooms – Fewer distractions, more focused learning.

Protect Your Tax Dollars – Responsible, common-sense spending.

Encourage Critical Thinking – Challenge students to question and apply logic.

Teach Real-World Skills – Financial literacy, problem-solving, and responsibility.

Support Creative Extracurriculars – Inspire leadership, teamwork, and innovation.

Build a Culture of Respect – Promote hard work, collaboration, and mutual respect.

Empower Parents – Give families a real voice in their child’s education.

Our Successes

Leadership & Governance
  • Hired a new Superintendent to unify the district and strengthen leadership.
  • Saved nearly $190,000 in two years through oversight and auditing of legal fees.
  • District goals shaped by staff, students, and the community.
  • Monthly progress reports ensure accountability and transparency.
Academics & Innovation
  • Introduced a no cell phone policy before it became a state mandate (#BePresent).
  • Expanded Career Academy with new technical programs.
  • Piloted Zello pathways program with resume tools and job postings.
  • Monthly “School Spotlight” reports from administrators.
  • Provided American flags in classrooms that lacked them.
Athletics & Extracurriculars
  • Expanded sports: Bowling, Lacrosse, and Volleyball.
  • Created Athletic Passes to cap family attendance costs.
  • Reduced Pay-to-Play fees by 50% with multi-sport caps.
  • Introduced therapy dogs to support student well-being.
Operations & Community Support
  • Negotiated a new 3-year contract with SWEA.
  • Eliminated school fees at the start of the year.
  • Uploaded Board meetings online for transparency.
  • Added an additional nurse to support student health.

Join Us in Shaping the Future
